⚡ AWS CLOUD EXTENSION NOTE Months 7–10 extend your core stack to the cloud. Month 7 covers AWS foundations and Solutions Architect concepts (IAM, VPC, EC2, Lambda) needed before touching data services. Month 8 covers the full AWS data stack (Glue, Redshift, Kinesis, Athena). Month 9 deploys your existing Airflow + Snowflake work onto AWS using MWAA, Snowpipe, and Terraform IaC. Month 10 targets the AWS Certified Data Engineer – Associate (DEA-C01) certification. Each project builds directly on the previous, so the 6-month foundation stays intact and is continuously extended — not replaced.
